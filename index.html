Our Mission

Leo District Council 325 E, Nepal is a non-profit youth initiative dedicated to enhancing the leadership skills of young individuals aged 30 and under. Based in Kathmandu, this organization engages youth through social work, seminars, webinars, and other activities. As a partner of Lions District 325 E, Nepal, Leo District Council 325 E provides a platform for young people to develop their skills and network with successful professionals across various sectors.

The organization offers young individuals the chance to connect with energetic peers, who are seen as the future leaders of tomorrow, and collaborate to drive positive change. Focused on empowering youth and nurturing their leadership potential, Leo District Council 325 E works closely with the Lions, who serve as mentors and guides for the young participants involved in the project.

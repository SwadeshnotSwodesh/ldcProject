Our Mission

Leo District Council 325 E, Nepal is a non-profit youth initiative dedicated to enhancing the leadership skills of young individuals aged 30 and under. Based in Kathmandu, the organization engages youth through social work, seminars, webinars, and other activities. As a partner of Lions District 325 E, Nepal, Leo District Council 325 E provides a platform for young people to develop their skills and network with successful professionals across various sectors.

The organization offers young individuals the chance to connect with energetic peers, who are seen as the future leaders of tomorrow, and collaborate to drive positive change. Focused on empowering youth and nurturing their leadership potential, Leo District Council 325 E works closely with the Lions, who serve as mentors and guides for the young participants involved in the project.




Our History

The Leo District Council 325 E, MD 325, Nepal was established on July 1st, 2022, following the bifurcation of four districts into fifteen districts. Serving as a youth project under the Lions District 325 E, Nepal, it stands as one of the districts among the fifteen within the Multiple District 325, Nepal. With a commitment to fostering youth leadership and community service, the council has seen dedicated leadership over the years. Leo Dolma Shrestha served as the inaugural District President for the Lionistic Year 2022/23, followed by Leo Nilisha Tuladhar for L.Y. 2023/24. Presently, Leo Reyaz Gautam holds the position of District President for L.Y. 2024/25, embodying the motto "Bona-Fide Impact."

The district consists of a network of sixteen clubs currently comprising over 400 members, organized into two zones and one region, under the committee of twenty-two elected and nominated board members. Operating primarily within the Kathmandu area, the council's core focus lies on service activities, skill development, sustainability, and societal impact, with a vision to extend these efforts nationwide. Among its member clubs are community-oriented organizations as well as institutional clubs each contributing to a broader reach for impactful service throughout the community and the nation.

The Leo Pledge


I pledge of my hands, extended and open to help those in need.

I pledge of my heart, reach for it, and it will be touched.

I pledge of my ears to hear another's outcry, my eyes to see the plight of others, my knowledge to bring a man close to his dreams.

I pledge of myself for the betterment of my community and my country.
